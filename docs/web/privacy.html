Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Hops: Daily Word Puzzle ("Hops", "we", "us") is a daily puzzle game. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What We Collect

Hops uses anonymous authentication — you are never asked for your name, email address, or any personal information to play.

  • Anonymous user ID — a randomly generated identifier created on your device when you first launch the app. It is not linked to your name, email, or any other personal information.
  • Display name — a player name you choose (optional). Defaults to a randomly generated name like "Player4823". This is the only user-provided data we store.
  • Gameplay data — the paths you take through puzzles, your completion time, and your score. Used to calculate your XP, streak, and leaderboard position.
  • Progress data — your XP total, current streak, and unlocked categories. Stored so your progress persists across sessions.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No email address or contact information
  • No precise or approximate location
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
  • No browsing history or data from other apps
  • No payment or financial information
  • No health or fitness data

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to make the game work:

  • Saving your progress, streak, and XP between sessions
  • Displaying leaderboards for daily puzzles
  • Improving puzzle quality over time based on aggregated (not individual) gameplay patterns

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers or third-party analytics services.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is stored in the United States. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your anonymous account and gameplay data is retained for as long as you use the app. To request deletion of your data, contact us at the email below and we will remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Children

Hops is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
