# RabbitHole — App Marketing Context

## App Overview
**Name:** RabbitHole
**Bundle ID:** com.benmattes.rabbithole
**Platform:** iOS
**Category:** Games → Puzzle
**Monetization:** Free (no IAP at launch)

## What It Is
A daily puzzle game where players connect two concepts (Start → End) by tracing a path through a web of intermediate "bubble" nodes. The fewer hops, the better. Knowledge beats speed — scoring rewards finding the right nodes in the right order, not just clicking fast.

## Target Audience
- Fans of Wordle, NYT Connections, Sporcle
- Trivia and pub quiz enthusiasts
- Ages 18–45, curious generalists
- People who enjoy a quick daily brain challenge

## Core Differentiators
- **Knowledge > speed** — scoring system rewards correct nodes, not just fast fingers
- **25+ topic categories** — Movies, Soccer, History, Science, Philosophy, Space, Hip-Hop, Literature, Comics, Food, and more
- **Difficulty levels** — Easy / Medium / Hard, unlocked as you level up
- **Hints that help, not spoil** — 4 hint types (Connection, Shuffle, Flash, Reveal)
- **Daily format** — one puzzle per category per day, fresh every morning

## Positioning
"The daily puzzle that rewards what you know."

Closer to a trivia-graph game than a word game. Players feel smart when they know a shortcut others missed.
